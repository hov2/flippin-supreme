About Flippin Supreme
Long before the first line of code or wireframe, it was decided that Flippin' Supreme would find its niche as a first-price auction platform. Sometimes known as a blind auction, a bidder typically submits an offer on a lot after some due diligence (without knowing other offer amounts from other bidders). This simplifies the overall auction process, eliminates bidding wars and frowned-upon practices such as "auction sniping". In a first-price auction, since the bidder is making his/her decision based solely on information that is avaiable and not on emotion, the result is a more just system.
Peers such as eBay and StockX offer multiple products from a multitude of brands, which often lead to longer turnaround times for sellers and buyers. Hence, Flippin' Supreme is a Supreme-product-focused site only. Flippin' Supreme is a proof-of-concept that will help alleviate some of the ongoing issues with current models of direct procurement, consignment, and traditional auction. While we are a new online auction platform strictly for "flipping" Supreme merchandise, we'll strive to become an influential market maker in this niche retail space of urban streewear.
